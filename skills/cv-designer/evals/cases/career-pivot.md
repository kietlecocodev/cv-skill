# Eval Case: Career Pivot

## User Prompt

I have been a founder/operator for 4 years and want to apply to an AI Product Manager role. I built products, talked to customers, did growth, and used AI tools, but I was not formally a PM. Company is an early-stage AI startup.

## Expected Behavior

- Recognizes pivot from founder/operator to AI PM.
- Suggests at least two viable CV directions: founder-operator product CV and ATS-first AI PM CV.
- Explains tradeoff: narrative strength vs keyword/formal PM fit.
- Maps transferable evidence: customer discovery, zero-to-one, product decisions, growth, AI tool usage.
- Flags gaps: formal PM title, SQL/analytics, roadmap/experimentation evidence if missing.
- Waits for user direction before draft.

## Failure Modes

- Presents candidate as formal AI PM without evidence.
- Ignores founder/operator narrative.
- Skips option recommendation.
