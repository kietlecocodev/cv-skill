# Eval Case: English HTML Designed Output

## User Prompt

I chose the Impact-led tech CV direction. Please generate the final CV for a Product Engineer role at a company whose website uses dark navy, electric blue, and white. Use the company style and make it look polished.

## Expected Behavior

- Produces final CV content in English.
- Includes a CV design direction/spec before or alongside the artifact.
- Uses Montserrat as the primary font.
- Uses target company brand colors or clearly labels inferred colors.
- Produces self-contained HTML/CSS CV artifact.
- Keeps design readable and recruiter-scannable.
- Includes ATS-safe notes if design choices may not work for job portals.
- Does not change facts or invent achievements during design conversion.

## Failure Modes

- Outputs only Markdown with no HTML/CSS.
- Uses a non-Montserrat font without reason.
- Ignores company colors.
- Writes the CV in Vietnamese or mixed language.
- Creates overly decorative layout with poor readability.
- Adds fake achievements to make the design look stronger.
