# Intake Guide

## Required Inputs

Accept any mix of:

1. Candidate CV or experience history as text/PDF.
2. Target JD as text/PDF/link.
3. Target company website or company context.

## Ask Before Drafting If Missing

Ask for missing critical information when it blocks useful tailoring:

- target role title and seniority,
- target JD or role description,
- company website or basic company context,
- candidate's recent roles and strongest achievements,
- constraints: one-page/two-page, geography, language, visa, remote/on-site, confidentiality.

## Candidate Extraction Checklist

Extract:

- roles, companies, dates, seniority,
- achievements and metrics,
- tech stack/tools/platforms,
- products/domains/customers,
- projects, portfolio, GitHub, publications,
- education/certifications,
- leadership scope,
- missing metrics,
- claims that need confirmation,
- sensitive/confidential details to avoid exposing.

## JD Extraction Checklist

Extract:

- must-have requirements,
- nice-to-have requirements,
- responsibilities,
- keywords and repeated terms,
- seniority signals,
- domain/product signals,
- leadership/collaboration expectations,
- implicit priorities such as speed, scale, compliance, AI, growth, or customer ownership.

## Company Extraction Checklist

From company website/context, infer:

- business model,
- product/service category,
- stage and market,
- customer type,
- culture/tone,
- likely hiring signals,
- CV tone implications.

## Partial Context Rule

If user wants to proceed with partial context, label assumptions and risks. Do not silently fill unknowns.
