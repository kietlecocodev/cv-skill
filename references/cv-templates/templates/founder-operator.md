# Founder Operator CV Template

## Target Roles

Founder, early-stage operator, chief of staff, product/business operations, growth lead, startup generalist, venture builder, entrepreneur-in-residence.

## When to Use

Use when the JD values zero-to-one ownership, ambiguity, product-market insight, customer development, growth, fundraising/partnerships, revenue ownership, or cross-functional execution.

## Section Order

1. Header
2. Founder/operator summary
3. Core operating skills
4. Founder/operator experience
5. Selected ventures/projects
6. Earlier experience/education

## Reconstructed Skeleton

### Summary

Founder/operator with experience taking <product/business> from <stage> to <outcome>. Strong in <customer discovery/growth/product/ops/fundraising/partnerships> with evidence of owning ambiguous problems end to end.

### Core Skills

- Startup: customer discovery, MVP scoping, GTM, fundraising, partnerships, hiring
- Product/growth: activation, retention, pricing, experiments, analytics
- Operations: process design, vendor management, finance/ops, cross-functional execution
- Technical fluency: APIs, no-code/automation, analytics, AI tools, developer collaboration where truthful

### Experience Bullet Pattern

- Built/launched <product/business/process> from <starting point>, reaching <revenue/users/partners/efficiency outcome>.
- Validated <market/customer hypothesis> through <research/sales/experiments>, leading to <pivot/launch/growth decision>.
- Owned <function> across <scope>, reducing <cost/time/risk> or increasing <growth/revenue/adoption>.

## Bullet Style

Show ownership, constraints, speed, learning, and measurable outcomes. Translate founder work into language relevant to the target role.

## Optimization Goal

Make non-linear startup experience legible to recruiters: scope, proof, decisions, and outcomes.

## Pitfalls

- Sounding unfocused or inflated.
- Listing too many responsibilities without evidence.
- Overclaiming fundraising, revenue, or traction.
- Failing to connect founder experience to the target JD.
