# Quantified Impact

## Actionable Rule

Use metrics when real and explainable; when metrics are unavailable, use concrete observable outcomes instead of inventing numbers.

## Why It Matters

Numbers help recruiters judge scope and credibility, but fake precision creates interview risk.

## Safe Examples

- Reduced API latency by 38%.
- Supported 120k monthly active users.
- Cut weekly reporting time from 6 hours to 45 minutes.
- Shipped dashboard adopted by sales leadership across 3 regions.

## Unsafe Examples

- Improved performance significantly.
- Increased revenue by 300% without source or ownership clarity.
- Added precise metrics the candidate cannot explain.
- Using vanity metrics unrelated to target role.
