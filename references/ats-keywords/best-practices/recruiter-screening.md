# Recruiter Screening

## Actionable Rule

Make role fit visible in the top third of the CV: target title, current/recent relevant scope, strongest skills, and measurable impact.

## Why It Matters

Recruiters often scan quickly before deciding whether to read deeply. Clarity of title, dates, companies, outcomes, and relevance reduces friction.

## Safe Examples

- Summary names role family and credible domain.
- Recent role bullets show outcomes aligned with JD.
- Skills section is grouped and relevant.
- Dates, titles, company names, and links are easy to parse.

## Unsafe Examples

- Dense paragraphs with no scan path.
- Internal acronyms without context.
- Vague seniority claims.
- Important relevant work buried below unrelated history.
