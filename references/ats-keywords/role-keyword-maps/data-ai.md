# Data and AI Keyword Map

## Target Role Family

Data scientist, ML engineer, AI engineer, analytics engineer, applied scientist.

## How to Use

Use these keywords only when they truthfully match the candidate's experience or the target JD. Prefer exact JD terms when truthful, then support important keywords in experience bullets.

## Core Hard-Skill Keywords

- Python, SQL, R, statistics, machine learning, deep learning, LLMs, NLP, computer vision, feature engineering, data pipelines, MLOps, model evaluation, dashboards

## Domain/System Keywords

experimentation, A/B testing, data quality, ETL/ELT, embeddings, retrieval, forecasting, recommendations, classification, monitoring

## Leadership or Collaboration Keywords

research communication, stakeholder analysis, product analytics, decision support, cross-functional data partnership

## Evidence Verbs

Modeled, analyzed, trained, evaluated, deployed, cleaned, engineered, forecasted, visualized, automated, monitored.

## Misuse Warnings

- Do not list skills the candidate cannot defend in interviews.
- Do not add terms only because they appear in the JD.
- Do not confuse exposure with ownership.
- Do not stuff keywords without evidence in experience or projects.
