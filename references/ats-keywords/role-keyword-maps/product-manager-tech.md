# Product Manager Tech Keyword Map

## Target Role Family

Product manager, technical product manager, platform PM, growth PM, AI PM.

## How to Use

Use these keywords only when they truthfully match the candidate's experience or the target JD. Prefer exact JD terms when truthful, then support important keywords in experience bullets.

## Core Hard-Skill Keywords

- roadmap, product discovery, user research, prioritization, experimentation, A/B testing, SQL, analytics, metrics, API, platform, developer experience, GTM, launch planning

## Domain/System Keywords

activation, retention, conversion, adoption, churn, pricing, funnels, cohorts, customer segmentation, product-market fit

## Leadership or Collaboration Keywords

stakeholder management, executive communication, cross-functional leadership, engineering/design/data partnership

## Evidence Verbs

Led, prioritized, launched, validated, analyzed, aligned, shipped, improved, scaled, discovered, defined.

## Misuse Warnings

- Do not list skills the candidate cannot defend in interviews.
- Do not add terms only because they appear in the JD.
- Do not confuse exposure with ownership.
- Do not stuff keywords without evidence in experience or projects.
