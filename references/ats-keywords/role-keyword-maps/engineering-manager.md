# Engineering Manager Keyword Map

## Target Role Family

Engineering manager, senior EM, software EM, platform EM, head of engineering.

## How to Use

Use these keywords only when they truthfully match the candidate's experience or the target JD. Prefer exact JD terms when truthful, then support important keywords in experience bullets.

## Core Hard-Skill Keywords

- team leadership, hiring, coaching, performance management, roadmap execution, delivery, technical strategy, architecture review, incident management, Agile, Scrum, Kanban

## Domain/System Keywords

reliability, platform strategy, developer productivity, delivery predictability, operational excellence, security, cloud, budget/vendor management

## Leadership or Collaboration Keywords

cross-functional alignment, stakeholder management, executive communication, conflict resolution, career development

## Evidence Verbs

Managed, hired, coached, aligned, delivered, improved, reduced, scaled, mentored, planned, resolved, established.

## Misuse Warnings

- Do not list skills the candidate cannot defend in interviews.
- Do not add terms only because they appear in the JD.
- Do not confuse exposure with ownership.
- Do not stuff keywords without evidence in experience or projects.
