# Sources

Accessed date: 2026-06-09

## Source List

### 1. How to Write an ATS Resume That Lands Interviews

- URL: https://www.jobscan.co/blog/ats-resume/
- Publisher/source: Jobscan
- Useful for: ATS formatting, keyword matching, job title alignment, skills/core competencies sections, achievement bullets.
- Notes: Recommends tailoring to each JD, mirroring truthful employer terminology, putting target title near top when accurate, using dedicated skills sections, and supporting keywords with context.
- Claim caution: Vendor claims about recruiter keyword filters should be treated as directional, not universal scoring rules.

### 2. How To Write an ATS-Friendly Resume

- URL: https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/resumes-cover-letters/ats-resume
- Publisher/source: Indeed Career Guide
- Useful for: standard headings, simple formatting, keyword extraction from JD, avoiding tables/columns/headers/footers.
- Notes: Emphasizes parsing risk from complex formatting and balancing ATS keywords with recruiter readability.
- Claim caution: ATS behavior varies by employer and implementation.

### 3. Resume Examples Library

- URL: https://www.tealhq.com/resume-examples
- Publisher/source: Teal
- Useful for: role-specific wording, resume tailoring, broad example taxonomy.
- Notes: Use as supporting source for role framing and keyword families; do not copy templates.
- Claim caution: Treat optimization advice as guidance rather than guaranteed ATS scoring.

### 4. Software Engineer Resume Examples

- URL: https://resumeworded.com/software-engineer-resume-examples
- Publisher/source: Resume Worded
- Useful for: engineering keywords, action verbs, quantified bullet examples, seniority signals.
- Notes: Supports role keyword maps for engineering and the importance of metrics, architecture, projects, and technical ownership.
- Claim caution: Example quality signals are useful, but exact wording should be reconstructed.

### 5. Engineering Manager Resume Examples

- URL: https://resumeworded.com/engineering-manager-resume-examples
- Publisher/source: Resume Worded
- Useful for: leadership keywords, Agile/project management terms, team scope, business impact.
- Notes: Useful for EM role keyword maps and recruiter-readable leadership outcomes.
- Claim caution: Role expectations vary by company size and engineering culture.

### 6. Data Scientist Resume: Elements, Examples, and Tips

- URL: https://www.coursera.org/articles/data-scientist-resume
- Publisher/source: Coursera Career Guide
- Useful for: data/AI keyword families, skills section, project/portfolio emphasis, certification placement.
- Notes: Supports clean formatting, applied experience, ML/statistics/Python/SQL keywords, and tailoring to role.
- Claim caution: Not an ATS vendor; use for resume content patterns more than parser claims.
